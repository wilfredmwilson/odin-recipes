Laddu Recipe




Description

Laddu or laddoo is a spherical sweet from the Indian subcontinent made of various ingredients and sugar syrup or jaggery.


    Ingredients

  • 3 cup sugar
  • 2 cup water
  • 2 1/2 cup gram flour (besan)
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon green cardamom
  • 2 pinch baking soda

  3. Further, add water slowly, and make smooth flowing consistency batter. if the batter is watery, then the boondi will turn flat. and if it is too thick, then the boondi will be formed with a tail.
